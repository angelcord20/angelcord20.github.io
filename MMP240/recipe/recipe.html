Mangu

Typical exquiste plate from the Dominican Republic that in fact I'm eating as I type this.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium (blank)s green plantains
  • 1 quart water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced white onion
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 cup sliced Anaheim peppers

    • Directions

      1. Place the plaintains and water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, and cook 20 minutes, until plantains are tender but slightly firm. Drain, reserving 1 cup of the liquid. Cool plantains, and peel.
      2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and saute the onion until tender.
      3. In a bowl, mash the plantains with the reserved liquid and salt. Transfer to a food processor, mix in the peppers, and puree. Serve the pureed plantain mixture topped with the onions.
